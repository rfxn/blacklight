Case lifecycle manager. Owns the open/close/reopen/hold state machine, INDEX row maintenance, and the agentic-minutes format (status + next-step + ask) for blacklight investigations.

Activate this skill whenever a lifecycle transition is in scope — open, close, reopen, hold — or whenever the operator needs a status summary with one next step and one blocking question, even if the request does not literally say "lifecycle" or "status."

Prefer synthesizing-evidence for cross-stream correlation; prefer authoring-incident-briefs for the final narrative brief.

Examples: "Open CASE-0042, init hypothesis.md and INDEX row" / "Close CASE-0042 after open-questions resolved" / "Emit agentic-minutes: status=active, next=observe.log_apache, ask=retention window?"
